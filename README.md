# 📊 Vrinda Store Sales Report 2022

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Vrinda Store Sales Report 2022** is an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard developed to analyze annual sales performance and uncover meaningful business insights. The dashboard enables users to explore sales trends, customer demographics, product categories, order status, sales channels, and state-wise performance through interactive slicers.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To analyze the 2022 sales data of Vrinda Store and provide actionable insights that help understand customer behavior, sales trends, and business performance for better decision-making.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- 📅 Monthly Sales Trend Analysis
- 💰 Order vs Sales Performance
- 👨‍🦰 Men vs Women Purchase Analysis
- 📦 Order Status Distribution
- 🏆 Top 5 Performing States
- 👥 Customer Age vs Gender Analysis
- 🛒 Sales Channel Performance
- 🎛 Interactive Slicers for:
  - Month
  - Category
  - Ship-State

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Women contributed a significantly higher percentage of total purchases than men.
- Amazon emerged as the leading sales channel.
- Maharashtra and Karnataka were among the highest-performing states.
- Most orders were successfully delivered.
- Adult customers generated the highest number of purchases.
- Monthly sales showed noticeable variations throughout the year.
